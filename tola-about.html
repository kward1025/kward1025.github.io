BIO

An East Coast native, Tola K. was born in the City of Charm. At the tender age of three, her family relocated to New Jersey, where she would live and grow up for the rest of her adolescent years. Just before turning eighteen, Tola K. moved back to Baltimore, Maryland to attend college at The Johns Hopkins University. She attained her Bachelor of Arts in Romance Languages with a minor concentration in Theater Arts Studies.

A natural entertainer at heart with a fiery passion for competition, Tola always enjoyed extracurriculars, especially theater and drama clubs. She always loved the stage, or just any opportunity to perform for a crowd! After graduating from college, she knew that it was only a matter of time before she would get to spread her wings and soar in a city that never sleeps. She wasted no time in turning those dreams into a reality.

Less than a year after graduating from Johns Hopkins, Tola K.  moved to New York city to pursue her acting career. Furthering her education while there, she completed a One-year "Acting for Film" conservatory at the New York Film Academy. After numerous features in stage productions , including off-broadway and film projects produced by the likes of NYU, NYFA, Brooklyn College and other independent producing companies. In the Fall of 2016,  Tola K. decided to attend Graduate school in Las Vegas, Nevada to pursue a Masters degree. In May of 2019, she graduated with an MFA in Performance/Acting. Tola currently resides in Los Angeles , California.
