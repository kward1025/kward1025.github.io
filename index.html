Onward Web Development

Mission

To create affordable, professional, and creative websites that assist actors and creative atists with becoming "bookable".

Creator

Onward Tech was founded by Kavon Ward in 2019. Kavon is an actor and poet who uses her passion for coding and designing websites to help struggling actors and creative artists build their artist/performance websites. As an actor, Kavon realized that there was a void needing to be filled as aspiring actors and artists are typically underpaid and often over-charged for headshots, demo reels, websites etc.

Languages

Kavon began studying fromt end web development in early 2018, and specializes in HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
